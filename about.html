Fun Facts

One time, my car broke down the night before my first day of a new job--in a new town--and so I ended up running 30 miles in to work that next morning. As if that weren't enough, I did it all while carrying a backpack full of my work stuff (i.e. khakis, dress shoes, toiletries, notepads, etc.). Hands down the best sleep of my life was had that night!

In 2009, I did a Semester at Sea, sailing around the world in 108-days with ports-of-call in the Bahamas, Spain, Gilbraltar, Morocco, the Canary Islands, Senegal, Namibia, South Africa, Mauritius, India, Thailand, Cambodia, Vietnam, China, Japan, Hawaii, Guatemala, and the Panama Canal.

I love playing the guitar and learning about blues music history. I once drove the entire Mississippi Blues Highway (Highway 61), stopping at various historical markers along the way, including the legendary "Crossroads." 🎶

I once took a spontaneous road-trip with my rescue cat Jim, from Houston, Texas, to the summit of Pikes Peak (14,115'), Colorado (perfectly sane...). Jim has since passed on, but our fun memories like this I will cherish forever!
